ILAHIA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND THECHNOLOGY

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET), located in Muvattupuzha, Kerala, is a private, co-educational minority institution established in 2002 by the Ilahia Trust. As one of the first self-financing engineering colleges in the state, ICET is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU).
