DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER APPLICATION

The Computer Applications department at Ilahia College of Engineering (ICET) offers a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program, aiming to prepare students as dynamic professionals with a blend of theoretical and practical knowledge in computer applications, data analysis, and software development. The department provides technical seminars, workshops, and industrial visits to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world scenarios, alongside placement training to enhance students' employability in the IT sector and startups.
